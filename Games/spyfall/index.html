Main Menu

Players join using the big link at the top. When at least 3 players are connected, press START GAME to begin a round.

Quick Overview

  • Everyone except the spy(ies) gets the same secret location on their phone.
  • Spies do not know the location, but they know they are spies.
  • Players ask each other questions out loud to find the spy(ies).
  • When you press BEGIN VOTE, everyone votes who they think the spy(ies) are.
  • Ties cause a new vote with only the tied players as options.
  • Scores never go below 0 and carry over between rounds.
Need at least 3 players to start the game.
