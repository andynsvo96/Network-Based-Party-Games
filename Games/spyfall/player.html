How to Play Spyfall

  • Everyone except one or more spies shares the same secret location.
  • Spies know only that they are spies; they do not know the location.
  • Players ask each other questions out loud about the location.
  • Spies try to blend in and guess the location without being caught.
  • When voting starts, you select who you think the spy(ies) are.
  • Points are awarded or deducted based on whether the group correctly finds the spy(ies).
